How to Use Poker Odds Master

×

Basic Usage

1. Select cards for your hand by clicking on them in the card deck below.

2. Add community cards by selecting them after your hand is complete.

3. Click "Calculate Odds" to see your probability of winning.

Adding Opponent Cards

1. Click on any player position on the table.

2. Enter their name and select their cards.

3. Mark them as active or folded.

4. Choose whether their cards are visible or hidden.
